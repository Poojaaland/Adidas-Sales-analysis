📊 Adidas Sales Performance & Profitability Dashboard | End-to-End Analysis

Excited to share my Power BI Sales Performance Dashboard project, where I analyzed Adidas sales data and transformed it into actionable business insights.

🚀 Project Overview

 Developed an interactive Power BI Sales Performance Dashboard to analyze Adidas sales data across products, regions, retailers, sales methods, and time periods. The project focuses on transforming raw sales transactions into actionable business insights by tracking revenue, operating profit, sales volume, target achievement, and month-over-month growth to support data-driven decision-making.

🔑 Key Business Insights

• Generated 899.9M total sales with 332.1M operating profit, indicating strong overall profitability

 • West region is the highest contributor to sales, while Midwest shows growth opportunities
 
 • West Gear and Foot Locker are top-performing retailers in terms of revenue and profit
 
 • Sales show clear seasonal trends, with peaks during mid-year and recovery toward year-end
 
 • Certain product categories deliver higher margins, guiding product-level focus
 
 • Sales targets were significantly exceeded, highlighting strong business performance
 

📈 Dashboard Highlights

✔ Executive-level KPI cards for Sales, Profit, Quantity & Target Achievement

 ✔ Retailer-wise sales vs operating profit comparison
 
 ✔ Region-wise sales distribution analysis
 
 ✔ Product-level margin and performance analysis
 
 ✔ Month-over-Month growth tracking with trend indicators

 ✔ Sales method contribution to total units sold

 
🛠 Tools & Technologies
• Power BI – Interactive dashboards, DAX measures & data modeling

 • DAX – MoM growth, MTD calculations, KPIs & ranking logic
 
 • Excel / CSV – Data cleaning and preprocessing
